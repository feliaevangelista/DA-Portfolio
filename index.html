About Me

An enthusiastic, detail-oriented business management graduate and aspiring data analyst who is looking for an opportunity in a supportive company which enables me to explore and become expert in data analytics. Completed an intensive data analytics course and have worked on an end-to-end retail banking dataset project to translate data into insights and give actionable recommendations to improve customer retention, customer experience, and maximize marketing campaigns. Along with my business background and solid skills of data analytics ground knowledge, I’m eager to leverage data and confident to pursue the data analyst career.
